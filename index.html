INSTALL Add to Your Phone

💜 iPhone (Safari)
  1. Open this app in Safari
  2. Tap the Share button 📤 at the bottom
  3. Scroll down & tap “Add to Home Screen”
  4. Tap Add in the top right
  5. Find Bill Tracker on your home screen

Must use Safari. Chrome on iPhone does not support Add to Home Screen.

🟢 Android (Chrome)
  1. Open this app in Chrome
  2. Tap the three-dot menu ⋮ top right
  3. Tap “Add to Home screen”
  4. Tap Add to confirm
  5. Find Bill Tracker on your home screen

Works on Chrome, Edge, or Samsung Internet.
